Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated / 最后更新日期: 02/03/2026

Advanced Improviser (the "App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy outlines how your data is handled.
Advanced Improviser（以下简称“本应用”）在设计时充分考虑了您的隐私。本政策概述了您的数据处理方式。

1. Data Collection / 数据收集

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers. All audio processing, waveform visualization, and loop point calculations are performed locally on your device.
本应用不会收集、存储或向外部服务器传输任何个人信息或用户数据。所有音频处理、波形可视化和循环点计算均在您的设备本地完成。

2. File Access and Storage / 文件访问与存储

The App requires permission to access audio files on your device to enable playback and editing features. Loop points and region data are saved locally on your device to ensure persistence when you reopen a file. This data is not shared with any third parties.
本应用需要访问您设备上音频文件的权限，以实现播放和编辑功能。循环点和区域数据保存在您的本地设备上，以确保您重新打开文件时数据依然存在。这些数据不会与任何第三方共享。

3. Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking software.
本应用不使用第三方分析、广告网络或追踪软件。

4. Children’s Privacy / 儿童隐私

Because the App does not collect any personal data, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
由于本应用不收集任何个人数据，因此适用于所有年龄段的用户。我们不会故意收集儿童的信息。

5. Changes to This Policy / 本政策的修订

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
我们可能会不时更新我们的隐私政策。任何修订都将通过更新本页面顶部的“最后更新日期”来体现。

6. Contact Us / 联系我们

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: sascus@gmail.com
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：sascus@gmail.com
